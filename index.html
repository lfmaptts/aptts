Zero-shot TTS (Extension of our work)

Below is an extended version of our model. Key changes are below.

  • Data scale-up: Public datasets — Korean 3.9k hours; English 6.5k hours.
  • Model size: 0.33B parameters.
  • Training pipeline: ProReFlow applied before adversarial post-training.
  • CFG: For faster inference, we used standardCFG instead of DualCFG — similar to F5-TTS & VoicBox. HybridCFG strategy is maintained.
